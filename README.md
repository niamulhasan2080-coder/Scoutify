# Scoutify# Scoutify Security & Trust Notice

Thank you for downloading Scoutify.

### Why does Android show a warning?

Scoutify is currently distributed directly by the developer and is not yet published on the Google Play Store. Because of this, some Android devices may display messages such as:

* "This file may be harmful"
* "Unknown app"
* "Blocked for your safety"

These warnings are shown automatically for many APK files downloaded outside official app stores and do not necessarily indicate that the application is unsafe.

### Your Privacy

Scoutify does not intentionally collect personal information beyond what is required for the app's functionality. User data is not sold to third parties.
